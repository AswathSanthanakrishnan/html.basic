Oreo Milkshake Recipe

Introduction

The Oreo milkshake is a delicious and refreshing treat that combines the classic flavors of Oreos and milk. It's perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth on a hot summer day.

How to Make an Oreo Milkshake

Follow these simple steps to make your own Oreo milkshake:

  1. Add the following ingredients to a blender:
    • 8 Oreo cookies
    • 2 cups of milk
    • 2 scoops of vanilla ice cream
  2. Blend the ingredients until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the milkshake into a glass.
  4. Optional: Top with whipped cream and crushed Oreos for garnish.
  5. Enjoy your delicious Oreo milkshake!

Ingredients

  • 8 Oreo cookies
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 scoops of vanilla ice cream

Measurements

Ingredient Measurement
Oreo cookies 8
Milk 2 cups
Vanilla ice cream 2 scoops
